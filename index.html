Our Story

Every great supply chain tells a story of connection connecting suppliers to customers, data to decisions, and operations to outcomes. We started PrimeChain Solutions because we saw too many of these connections breaking down. From our early roots in supply chain operations, we've grown to support clients across logistics, e-commerce, mining, retail, and professional services improving everything from inventory control to fleet performance.

We knew fixing these disconnections required more than just hands-on support it needed turning scattered information into clear answers. Today, we help businesses cut through the complexity, make better decisions, and get more value from their supply chain.

What sets us apart is our unique blend of logistics expertise, data science, and Six Sigma methodology. We don't just build technology we use technology to solve the real problems that keep supply chain leaders up at night.
